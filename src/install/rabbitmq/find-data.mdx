This service reports data to an [integration dashboard](/docs/integrations/new-relic-integrations/getting-started/infrastructure-integration-dashboards-charts).

These [event types](/docs/using-new-relic/data/understand-data/new-relic-data-types#events-new-relic) have attached metrics:

* [RabbitmqVhostSample](#vhostsample)
* [RabbitmqNodeSample](#nodesample)
* [RabbitmqExchangeSample](#exchangesample)
* [RabbitmqQueueSample](#queuesample)

Follow these steps to find your RabbitMQ data:

1. Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com) > Integrations & Agents** and type **RabbitMQ**.

2. Under **Dashboards**, click **RabbitMQ**.

   <img
     title="Install the RabbitMQ dashboards"
     alt="Installing the RabbitMQ dashboards"
     src="/images/infrastructure_screenshot-full_rabbitmq-dashboard.webp"
   />

3. A popup window opens.

4. Click **Edit** if you want to change the account.

5. Click **Setup RabbitMQ** or **Skip this step** if you already setup this datasource.

6. Click **View dashboard**, and see your RabbitMQ data in New Relic.

For more on how to find and use your data, see how to [understand integration data](/docs/infrastructure/integrations/find-use-infrastructure-integration-data). You can find a full list of metrics and their attributes at the bottom of this doc.
