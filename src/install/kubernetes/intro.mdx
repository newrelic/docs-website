New Relic's Kubernetes integration gives you full observability into the health and performance of your infrastructure. With this agent you can collect telemetry data from your cluster using several New Relic integrations such as the [Kubernetes events integration](/docs/kubernetes-pixie/kubernetes-integration/understand-use-data/kubernetes-events-integration/), the [Prometheus agent](/docs/infrastructure/prometheus-integrations/install-configure-prometheus-agent/install-prometheus-agent/), the [nri-kubernetes](https://github.com/newrelic/nri-kubernetes), and the [New Relic Logs Kubernetes plugin](/docs/logs/forward-logs/kubernetes-plugin-log-forwarding/).
