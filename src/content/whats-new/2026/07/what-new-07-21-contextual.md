We've introduced a new feature that integrates your custom dashboards as contextual information directly within Catalogs. 

This capability bridges the "what" (your services and infrastructure) with the "how" (your custom visualizations). Instead of losing time hunting across tools for the right view, you can now assign the dashboards that matter most to a specific service, application, or infrastructure resource. From then on, your team can reach the exact dashboard they need in a single click, right from the entity.

## Key benefits to your workflow:

  * **Quick access where it matters**: Your custom dashboards live right alongside the entities they describe, giving you immediate, contextual access to curated metrics.
  * **A truly centralized hub**: Stop jumping between different product experiences to find the right dashboard; Catalogs serves as your single source of truth and starting point.
  * **Faster resolution when it counts**: When an entity shows signs of distress, you can jump straight from Catalogs to the dashboards you've attached for deeper analysis, cutting down your mean time to resolution (MTTR) without losing context.

## How to get started

Go to your Applications or Infrastructure Catalogs and link your most important dashboards to their corresponding entities. Once added, they become a permanent part of each entity's context—providing a 1-click bridge from your starting point to the deep-dive views your team relies on.

To learn more, check out the [Catalogs documentation](https://docs.newrelic.com/docs/service-architecture-intelligence/catalogs/catalogs/).
