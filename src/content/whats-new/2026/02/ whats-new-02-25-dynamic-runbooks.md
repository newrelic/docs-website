New Relic Notebooks enables you to create structured documents with individual blocks for queries, visualizations and text. This helps facilitate collaborative data analysis and storytelling, investigation, and the saving and sharing of analytical workflows. (**Sign up for Preview**)

Notebooks is designed to help users sequentially build and refine queries, compare results to find anomalies, and document their investigation process for future reference and collaboration, turning temporary troubleshooting into dynamic, reusable runbooks.
