All new accounts (outside Japan) created on or after July 30, 2020 are on our newer usage-based pricing model and our newer user model.

If (i) your account was created prior to July 30, 2020; or (ii) you are a current New Relic K.K. customer (Japan): in addition to the [New Relic Pre-release policy](/docs/licenses/license-information/referenced-policies/new-relic-pre-release-policy), use of the Developer Edition of New Relic to the extent it is available is also subject to the following terms:

## Terms [#developer-edition]

* Non-production use only
* Up to $500 per month in total product usage across the New Relic platform
* Up to 2 users
