This is a glossary of terms that appear in contracts for our [New Relic usage-based pricing model](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-users/pricing-billing) (for a glossary for our original pricing, see [Original pricing definitions](/docs/licenses/license-information/product-definitions/legacy-product-definitions/).

<CollapserGroup>
  <Collapser
    id="account"
    title="Account"
  >
    **Account** refers to the online account or subaccounts that New Relic provides for customers to manage their use of the Products.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="buying-program"
    title="Buying Program"
  >
    **Buying Program** means the pricing plan identified on Customer's Order or Usage Plan, that may include details related to pricing commitments and billing, as further described in the Usage Plan. On your Order, the Buying Program may be identified as "Subscription."
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="commitment-term"
    title="Commitment Term"
  >
    **Commitment Term** means the non-cancelable, committed Subscription Term or Order Term for the Products.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="compute-capacity-unit"
    title="Compute Capacity Unit (CCU)"
  >
    Compute Capacity Unit ("CCU") means the compute capacity consumed for a successfully-run action, including loading a page, executing a query, evaluating an alert condition, and invoking an API call. CCU includes the queries, usage, and processes necessary to retrieve your data, perform the user functions, or serve up New Relic features.

    This unit of measure may indicate a data region, such as `EU`, associated with your CCU, as applicable.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="core-user"
    title="Core User"
  >
    A Core User is any user who can log into Customer's Account(s) and access the New Relic Product functionality described in an Order and the Documentation.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="customer-data"
    title="Customer Data"
  >
    **Customer Data** means the data, information, or content that Customer and its users send to an Account from the Software, the Customer Properties, or Third-Party Services.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="customer-properties"
    title="Customer Properties"
  >
    **Customer Properties** means Customer's websites, infrastructure, networks, mobile applications, or other systems, as well as Customer accounts on Third-Party Services.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="documentation"
    title="Documentation"
  >
    **Documentation** means the New Relic technical guides and documentation made available from the dedicated 'Documentation' page of the New Relic website.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="full-platform-user"
    title="Full Platform User"
  >
    A Full Platform User is any user who can log into Customer's Account(s) and access the New Relic Product functionality described in an Order and the Documentation.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="gb-ingested"
    title="GB Ingested"
  >
    A **GB Ingested** is a measurement of the volume of metrics, events, logs, traces, or other telemetry data sent to or generated by the Products for the benefit of the Customer, including from the Software, the Customer Properties, or Third-Party Services. GB Ingested also includes telemetry data from preview or pre-release versions of software.

    In this context, a GB is defined as 1 billion bytes.

    This unit of measure may indicate a data region, such as `EU`, associated with your GB Ingested, as applicable.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="gb-months"
    title="GB Month"
  >
    A GB Month is a measurement of the volume of data and length of time stored by the Products for the benefit of the Customer, including from the Software, the Customer Properties, or Third-Party Services. GB Months also includes telemetry data from preview or pre-release versions of software.

    The GB Month unit of measure is rounded up to the nearest integer.

    In this context, a GB is defined as 1 billion bytes.

    This unit of measure may indicate a data region, such as `EU`, associated with your GB Months, as applicable.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="incident-event"
    title="Incident event"
  >
    An **incident event** is an alerting event (open, closed, etc.) created by an alerting engine that is sent into the New Relic applied intelligence platform (non-unique) for de-duplication, flapping detection, smart suppression, enrichment, and correlation.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="list-price"
    title="List price"
  >
    List Price means the per-unit prices located in the Usage Plan.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="login-credentials"
    title="Login Credentials"
  >
    **Login Credentials** means the username, email address, password, or other personal information that is provided by a Customer user in order to manage an Account.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="monthly-provisined-user"
    title="Monthly Provisioned User"
  >
    A **Monthly Provisioned User** is any user who can log into Customer's Account(s) and access the New Relic Product functionality described in an Order and the Documentation.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="order"
    title="Order"
  >
    **Order** means the purchasing order for access to the Product, Service, or related services that: (1) is either executed by the Parties or entered into by you via self-service, and references this Agreement, or (2) is entered into by you and a Channel Partner.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="paid-terms-service"
    title="Paid Terms of Service"
  >
    **Paid Terms of Service** means the legal terms and conditions located at: [https://newrelic.com/termsandconditions/paid](https://newrelic.com/termsandconditions/paid).
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="products"
    title="Product(s)"
  >
    **Product(s)** mean the purchase of the New Relic subscription products described in the applicable Order and Usage Plan, and any updates, corrections, bug fixes, modifications, improvements, related services, new features, and functionality (made generally available to New Relic's customer base) thereto.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="renewal-date"
    title="Renewal Date"
  >
    **Renewal Date** means the renewal date identified in an Order or the day immediately succeeding the last day of your Commitment Term.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="software"
    title="Software"
  >
    **Software** means the distributed software, APIs, scripts, or other code proprietary to New Relic provided with the Products.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="terms"
    title="Terms"
  >
    **Terms** means the underlying Customer-New Relic agreement and the Order.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="third-parties"
    title="Third-Party Services"
  >
    **Third-Party Services** means any third party platform, add-on, service, or product not provided by New Relic and that a user integrates or enables for use with the Products, including third-party applications and plug-ins.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="unpaid-terms"
    title="Unpaid Terms of Service"
  >
    **Unpaid Terms of Service** means the legal terms and conditions located at: [https://newrelic.com/termsandconditions/unpaid](https://newrelic.com/termsandconditions/unpaid).
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="usage-plan"
    title="Usage Plan"
  >
    **Usage Plan** refers to the Service or Product subscription, pricing, invoicing related information, and product-specific terms (e.g. concurrent user account sessions) contained within the Documentation.
  </Collapser>
</CollapserGroup>

To learn more about this pricing model, see [New Relic usage-based pricing](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-users/pricing-billing).
