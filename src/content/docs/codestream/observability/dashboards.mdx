<Callout title="Feature availability" variant="important">
  This feature isn't available in all regions. Refer to the [data center and regional availability](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/account-setup/choose-your-data-center#feature-limitations-by-region) page to check if it's supported in your region and explore alternative solutions.
</Callout>

Dashboards are central to your New Relic experience, and you now have access to all your dashboards directly from your IDE. Look for the <DNT>**Dashboards**</DNT> icon in CodeStream's global navigation to search for any dashboard or access your favorites.

<img
  title="Dashboard search."
  alt="A screenshot displaying the ability to search for dashboards."
  src="/images/codestream_screenshot-full_search-dashbaords.webp"
/>

To open a dashboard, just click it. There's no need to switch to your web browser.
