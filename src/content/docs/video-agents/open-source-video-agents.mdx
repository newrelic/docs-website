New Relic has a variety of open-source tools for monitoring video. Some are in the category of experimental and some are community projects:

* iOS & tvOS
* Android Mobile and TV
* Chromecast
* Roku (device or TV)

For requirements, installation, and configuration information, see our [wiki](https://github.com/newrelic-experimental/video-documentation/wiki) on GitHub.
