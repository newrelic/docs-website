A budget establishes a spending limit for an account over a flexible timeframe (one to twelve months), helping you control cloud expenditures by monitoring them against this predefined limit.

<img title="Cost types highlighted on the Cloud Cost Intelligence budgets page" alt="Cost types highlighted on the Cloud Cost Intelligence budgets page" src="/images/cci_budgets.webp"/>

## Create budget

<Steps>

  <Step>
    ## Navigate to Budgets
      Go to **All Capabilities** > **Cloud Cost Intelligence** > **Budgets**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Configure budget

      1. Click **Create a budget**.
      2. Enter the following information:
        - **Budget name** - Descriptive name for your budget
        - Select setup type:

  <Step>
    ## Review and create

    Review the budget setup and click **Create**.

        <Callout variant="important">
          **Cost Intelligence** provides average compute cost over the last 3 months based on actual spend in the past 90 days.
