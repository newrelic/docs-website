## How do I access capabilities currently in preview? [#preview-access]

Some CCI capabilities, such as the FinOps Agent and CCI Alerts, are available as preview features before general availability. To enable them:

1. Log in to your New Relic account.
2. In the bottom-left corner, select your **User Profile** icon.
3. Go to **Administration > Previews and Trials**.
4. Locate the feature you want to enable:
   - **FinOps Agent Preview:** Toggle this on to enable natural language cost querying and advanced cloud spend analysis.
   - **CCI Alerts Preview:** Toggle this on to enable the ability to create and manage alerts directly within the CCI ecosystem.
5. Refresh your browser to see the new capabilities reflected in the Ask AI interface.
