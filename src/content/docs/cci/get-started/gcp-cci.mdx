Connect your Google Cloud Platform billing to Cloud Cost Intelligence to monitor and optimize costs. The integration uses service account-based authorization to securely access your GCP billing and pricing data. New Relic creates a dedicated service account with read-only access to your BigQuery datasets.

## Before you begin

You need:
  - **GCP project connected to New Relic infrastructure monitoring** - See [Connect Google Cloud Platform services to New Relic](/docs/infrastructure/google-cloud-platform-integrations/get-started/connect-google-cloud-platform-services-new-relic/)
  - **Active GCP billing account**
  - **BigQuery dataset** with:
      - Billing Export table
      - Cloud Pricing Export table
  - **Google Cloud Console access**

## Setup instructions

<Steps>
  <Step>
     ### Select New Relic account [#select-nr-account]

      1. Go to <DNT>[**one.newrelic.com**](https://one.newrelic.com) > **All Capabilities** > **Cloud Cost Intelligence - Google Cloud Platform**</DNT>.
      2. Select your account and click **Continue**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
     ### Provide GCP connection details [#gcp-connection-details]

      1. Enter your GCP billing and dataset information:
          - **Connection name** - Descriptive name
          - **Project ID** - Your GCP project ID
          - **Dataset name** - Your BigQuery dataset name
          - **Billing table name** - Your billing table name
          - **Pricing table name** - Your pricing table name
      2. Click **Continue**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
     ### Authorize access [#gcp-authorize-access]

      1. Copy the service account ID displayed.
      2. Open **Google Cloud Console** and grant **BigQuery DataViewer** role to the service account for your BigQuery billing tables.
      3. Return and click **Continue**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
     ### Create connection [#gcp-create-connection]

      Click **Create connection** to verify configuration and establish the integration.
  </Step>

  <Step>
     ### View your data [#gcp-view-data]

      Go to [**Intelligent summary**](/docs/cci/financial-intelligence/intelligent-summary) to view your GCP cost data.

        <Callout variant="important">
          First-time processing can take up to 12 hours.
        </Callout>
  </Step>
</Steps>
