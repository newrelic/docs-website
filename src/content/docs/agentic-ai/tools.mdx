New Relic AI provides various tools to help you manage and understand your data and issues. These tools integrate directly into the New Relic platform and other services, offering capabilities to streamline your observability tasks. The following table lists the available tools, their functions, and how you can access them.

<Callout variant="tip" title="tip">
Save cost and time by using slash commands (for example, `/Fetch Top Deviations`) to directly call tools, skipping New Relic AI's routing step.
</Callout>
