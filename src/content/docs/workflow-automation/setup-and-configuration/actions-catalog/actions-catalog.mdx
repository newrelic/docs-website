The New Relic actions catalog provides 46+ actions to automate your infrastructure and integrations. Orchestrate end-to-end processes by linking together actions that perform tasks in your cloud providers, communication tools, and New Relic accounts.

Actions follow a hierarchical naming convention: `company.product.action`
