Data ingest budgets help you control costs by setting monthly data limits (GB), tracking usage in real-time, and alerting you before you exceed your budget.

## Budget types by customer structure

The type of budgets you can create depends on your customer structure:

### For single-tenant customers

You can create:
- **Organization level budgets**: The top-level budget for your entire organization, tracking total data ingestion across all accounts
- **Account level budgets**: Budgets for individual accounts or groups of up to 5 accounts to allocate portions of your organization budget to specific teams, applications, or business units

### For multi-tenant customers

**Managing organizations** can create three types of budgets:
- **Company level budgets**: Track total data ingestion across all organizations in your multi-tenant setup
- **Organization level budgets**: Allocate portions of your company budget to individual managed organizations (one budget per organization, create multiple as needed)
- **Account level budgets**: Budgets for accounts within the managing organization (same functionality as single-tenant customers)

**Managed organizations** can't create budgets or access the data budgets UI.

<Callout variant="important">
If you are part of a managing organization, you can create budgets for any managed organization under your customer hierarchy. Use the organization selector to choose the organization when creating an organization budget.
</Callout>

## Prerequisites

To create, edit, or delete data ingest budgets, you need:

- Organization manager role (for managing organizations in multi-tenant setups) or equivalent admin permissions
- Paid data ingest subscription

<Callout variant="tip">
  All users with account access can view budgets and receive notifications if configured as destinations.
</Callout>

## Access Data Budgets

To create or manage any budget type:

1. Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/)**.
2. In the [user menu](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/general-account-settings/intro-account-settings), select **Administration** > **Data Management** > **Data Budgets**.

## Set up company-level budgets

<Callout variant="tip">
  Company-level budgets are only available for multi-tenant managing organizations.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Access company budget

    If you have a data ingestion contract commitment, your company-level budget appears automatically. Otherwise, select **Create company budget**.

    <Callout variant="important">
      Default company-level budgets are based on your monthly contract commitments but don't include notifications. You must configure thresholds and destinations to receive alerts.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set monthly budget limits

    1. Select **Manage limits** in the Company Budget section.
    2. In the **Set monthly budget limits** section, enter your monthly data limit in GB.

    <Callout variant="important">
      By default, this is configured based on your monthly contract commitments. You can adjust the amount based on your expected usage.
    </Callout>

    3. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the GB assigned to your company budget for a specific month, in the **Thresholds** section, select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications (for example, 75%, 90%, or 95%).
    2. To ensure notifications are sent when the budget exceeds the threshold, in the **Destinations** section, add at least one destination to be notified. You can choose from the following options:
       - **Email**: Enter the email addresses of New Relic users to notify about the budget.
       - **Webhook**: Enter the webhook URL to send notifications to a specific endpoint.
    3. Select **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set up organization-level budgets

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create an organization budget

    **For single-tenant customers**: If you have a contract commitment, your organization budget appears automatically. Otherwise, select **Create organization budget**.

    **For multi-tenant managing organizations**: After setting up your company-level budget, select **+ Add organization budget** to create budgets for managed organizations.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      For multi-tenant customers, use the organization selector to view and select the managed organization for your budget.
    </Callout>

    <Callout variant="important">
      Default organization budgets are based on your monthly contract commitments but don't include notifications. You must configure thresholds and destinations to receive alerts.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Select organization

    **For single-tenant customers**: Select **Manage limits** in the Organization Budget section.

    **For multi-tenant managing organizations**: Select one organization from the dropdown. Each organization level budget can contain only one organization.

    Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set monthly budget limits

    1. In the **Set monthly budget limits** section, enter your monthly data limit in GB.

    <Callout variant="important">
      For single-tenant customers, this is configured by default based on your monthly contract commitments. For multi-tenant customers, you'll see how many GB are remaining in your company budget and can allocate any amount from your company-level budget.
    </Callout>

    2. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the GB assigned to your organization budget for a specific month, in the **Thresholds** section, select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications (for example, 75%, 90%, or 95%).
    2. To ensure notifications are sent when the budget exceeds the threshold, in the **Destinations** section, add at least one destination to be notified. You can choose from the following options:
       - **Email**: Enter the email addresses of New Relic users to notify about the budget.
       - **Webhook**: Enter the webhook URL to send notifications to a specific endpoint.
    3. Select **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set up account-level budgets

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create a budget

    In the **Functional budgets** section, select **+ Add functional budget**.

    <Callout variant="important">
      **For multi-tenant customers**, this section displays:
      - **Organization level budgets** for managed organizations
      - **Account level budgets** for the managing organization

      Managed organizations can't create account level budgets.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Assign your budget

    1. Enter a name for your budget.
    2. From the dropdown list, select and assign up to 5 accounts to this budget.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      Accounts that already have budgets assigned will appear disabled in the selection list.
    </Callout>

    3. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set monthly budget limits

    1. In the **Set monthly budget limits** section, you'll see how many GB are remaining in your organization budget.
    2. Enter the amount of GB you want to allocate to this account-level budget.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      You can allocate more than your organization budget. This allows flexible planning but may result in overages.
    </Callout>

    3. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the GB assigned to this budget for a specific month, in the **Thresholds** section, select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications (for example, 75%, 90%, or 95%).

## Manage existing budgets

To manage an existing budget:

1. Locate the budget in the list and select the three-dot menu.
2. Choose from the following options:
   - **View details**: See detailed information about the budget, including utilization graphs, thresholds, notification settings, associated accounts, and data sources.
   - **Edit**: Modify the budget allocation, thresholds, or notification settings.
   - **Delete**: Remove the budget.

### View budget details

Select a budget name or choose **View details** from the three-dot menu to see:

- **Total allocation**: The total GB allocated to this budget
- **Utilization**: Visual graph showing usage over time with day-over-day breakdown
- **Thresholds**: The notification thresholds you've configured
- **Notification destinations**: Where alerts are sent when thresholds are exceeded
- **Associated accounts**: The accounts included in this budget
- **Data sources**: Information about data sources contributing to budget usage, categorized by source type
- **Budget time range**: The time period covered by this budget

## Best practices

- **Set realistic limits**: Base budgets on historical usage and expected growth.
- **Use multiple thresholds**: Set alerts at 75%, 90%, and 95% to allow time for action before exceeding your budget.
- **Allocate by team or project**: Use account-level budgets to track high-usage areas and assign accountability.
- **Review monthly**: Adjust budgets when usage patterns or monitoring capabilities change.
- **Analyze trends**: Use utilization graphs to identify spikes or anomalies in your data ingestion.
