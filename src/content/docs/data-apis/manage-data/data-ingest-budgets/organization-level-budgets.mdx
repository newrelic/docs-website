Effective cost management is crucial for organizations, and New Relic offers deep visibility into your data ingestion usage. Additionally, implementing proactive mechanisms to alert and stay within budget can help manage costs effectively across the organization.

## Set up company-level budgets

<Callout variant="tip">
  Company-level budgets are only available for multi-tenant managing organizations.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Access company budget

    If you have a data ingestion contract commitment, your company-level budget appears automatically. Otherwise, select **Create company budget**.

    <Callout variant="important">
      Default company-level budgets are based on your monthly contract commitments but don't include notifications. You must configure thresholds and destinations to receive alerts.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set monthly budget limits

    1. Select **Manage limits** in the Company Budget section.
    2. In the **Set monthly budget limits** section, enter your monthly data limit in GB.

    <Callout variant="important">
      By default, this is configured based on your monthly contract commitments. You can adjust the amount based on your expected usage.
    </Callout>

    3. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the GB assigned to your company budget for a specific month, in the **Thresholds** section, select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications (for example, 75%, 90%, or 95%).
    2. To ensure notifications are sent when the budget exceeds the threshold, in the **Destinations** section, add at least one destination to be notified. You can choose from the following options:
       - **Email**: Enter the email addresses of New Relic users to notify about the budget.
       - **Webhook**: Enter the webhook URL to send notifications to a specific endpoint.
    3. Select **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Set up organization-level budgets

<Steps>
  <Step>
    ### Create an organization budget

    **For single-tenant customers**: If you have a contract commitment, your organization budget appears automatically. Otherwise, select **Create organization budget**.

    **For multi-tenant managing organizations**: After setting up your company-level budget, select **+ Add organization budget** to create budgets for managed organizations.

    <Callout variant="tip">
      For multi-tenant customers, use the organization selector to view and select the managed organization for your budget.
    </Callout>

    <Callout variant="important">
      Default organization budgets are based on your monthly contract commitments but don't include notifications. You must configure thresholds and destinations to receive alerts.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Select organization

    **For single-tenant customers**: Select **Manage limits** in the Organization Budget section.

    **For multi-tenant managing organizations**: Select one organization from the dropdown. Each organization level budget can contain only one organization.

    Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set monthly budget limits

    1. In the **Set monthly budget limits** section, enter your monthly data limit in GB.

    <Callout variant="important">
      For single-tenant customers, this is configured by default based on your monthly contract commitments. For multi-tenant customers, you'll see how many GB are remaining in your company budget and can allocate any amount from your company-level budget.
    </Callout>

    2. Select **Next**.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the GB assigned to your organization budget for a specific month, in the **Thresholds** section, select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications (for example, 75%, 90%, or 95%).
    2. To ensure notifications are sent when the budget exceeds the threshold, in the **Destinations** section, add at least one destination to be notified. You can choose from the following options:
       - **Email**: Enter the email addresses of New Relic users to notify about the budget.
       - **Webhook**: Enter the webhook URL to send notifications to a specific endpoint.
    3. Select **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
