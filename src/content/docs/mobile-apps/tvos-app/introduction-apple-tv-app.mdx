The New Relic app for Apple TV provides easy access to your [New Relic Dashboards](/docs/query-your-data/explore-query-data/dashboards/introduction-dashboards) data. This is useful, for example, to display critical data on your kiosks, in your conference rooms, or anywhere you want your teams or customers to see data trends.

## Install and log in [#install-log-in]

[Additional log-in requirements](#account-reqs) may apply depending on your account type. Review the [New Relic Apple TV app release notes](/docs/release-notes/mobile-apps-release-notes/new-relic-tvos-release-notes) to make sure you have the latest version.

To install and log into the New Relic Apple TV app:

1. Download the app from the [Apple TV app store](https://support.apple.com/guide/tv/find-apps-atvb5a7dd799/tvos).
2. Log in with your New Relic username and password.

   OR

   Log in using your [New Relic iPhone app](/docs/mobile-apps/new-relic-mobile-apps/ios-app/introduction-ios-mobile-app) credentials by selecting **New Relic iPhone app > Accounts settings > TV icon** and entering the code displayed on the screen. [#log-in-via-iphone]

## App features [#features]

The New Relic Apple TV app is a companion app that enables you to view dashboards created via the New Relic One website and create dashboard slideshows. Information updates in real time, so you always have access to the latest events and metrics. Features include:

  <thead>
    <tr>

    <tr>
      <td>
        Run slideshow

      <td>

      </td>
    </tr>

    <tr>

        * Expand any dashboard widget to see full details.
        * Switch to dark mode. To switch from light to dark mode:

          * Activate Siri on the Apple TV and say **dark mode**.

          OR

          * Navigate to **Settings > General > Appearance** and select **Dark**. tvOS 11 and higher can switch between dark and light modes automatically, based on sunset and sunrise times.

## Data privacy [#privacy]

To make troubleshooting easier, New Relic's mobile apps only record the following information about you:

* User's email address associated with your New Relic account, including first and last name (for authentication purposes only)
* IP address
* Device ID

For more information, see our [Mobile data privacy and security documentation](/docs/mobile-monitoring/new-relic-mobile/get-started/security-mobile-apps).
