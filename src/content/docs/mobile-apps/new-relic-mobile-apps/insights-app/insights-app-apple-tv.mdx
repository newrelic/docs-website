The New Relic Insights app for Apple TV provides easy access to your [New Relic Insights](/docs/insights/new-relic-insights/understanding-insights/new-relic-insights) data. This is useful, for example, to display critical data on your kiosks, in your conference rooms, or anywhere you want your teams or customers to see data trends.

![apple-tv-tv.jpg](./images/apple-tv-tv.jpg "apple-tv-tv.jpg")

**New Relic Insights for Apple TV**: Search and view any of your account dashboards, favorite dashboards, or data apps, and create a slideshow to cycle through them on your display.

## Install and log in [#install-log-in]

[Additional log-in requirements](#account-reqs) may apply depending on your account type. Review the [Insights Apple TV app release notes](/docs/release-notes/mobile-apps-release-notes/insights-tvos-release-notes) to make sure you have the latest version.

To install and log into the Insights Apple TV app:

1. Download the app from the [Apple TV app store]\([https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT205354Apple](https://support.apple.com/en-us/HT205354Apple) TV).
2. Log in with your New Relic username and password.

   OR

   Log in using your [Insights iPhone app](/docs/mobile-apps/insights-app/features/insights-mobile-app) credentials by selecting **Insights iPhone app > Accounts > TV icon** and entering the code displayed on the screen.

## Data privacy [#privacy]

To make troubleshooting easier, New Relic's mobile apps only record the following information about you:

* User's email address associated with your New Relic account, including first and last name (for authentication purposes only)
* IP address
* Device ID

For more information, see our [Mobile data privacy and security documentation](/docs/mobile-monitoring/new-relic-mobile/get-started/security-mobile-apps).
