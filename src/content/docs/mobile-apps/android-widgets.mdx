## Add an Android widget [#add-widget]

Complete the following to add a widget:

1. Touch and hold an empty area until a modal appears.
2. Tap <DNT>**Widgets**</DNT>.
3. Search for the New Relic widget.
4. Tap and hold the widget to move it to the desired location on your device.
5. Select the account and time range you want to count active issues.
6. Long press and adjust size as needed.

The widget is added to your home screen with the account you are currently viewing, and time is set to last 30 minutes.

## Add a chart to a widget [#add-chart]

If you haven't already selected the chart you want to show from the New Relic app, then follow these steps:

1. Navigate to whatever chart you would like to see in the widget.
2. Tap on <DNT>**+ Create Widget**</DNT>.
3. Tap on the <DNT>**Add to home screen**</DNT> button that appears at the bottom.
4. Long press and adjust size as needed.
5. Tap on home screen widget to open the New Relic app to your <DNT>**Issue**</DNT> list.
