You can enable faceting when creating a new service level or editing an existing one.

## Before you begin [#before-you-begin]

To use faceted service levels, you need an existing [service level](/docs/service-level-management/intro-slm/) or the ability to create one.

## Enable faceting on a service level [#enable-faceting]

1. Go to <DNT>**[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Service Levels**</DNT>.

2. Select an existing Service Level to edit, or click <DNT>**+ Add a service level**</DNT>.

3. Complete the standard Service Level configuration steps:

   1. [Select the entity](/docs/service-level-management/create-slm/#select-sli-source).
   2. [Configure valid events and good/bad events queries](/docs/service-level-management/create-slm/#configure-queries).

4. In the <DNT>**Group by attributes (optional)**</DNT> step, from the <DNT>**FACET**</DNT> dropdown, select up to 2 attributes that are common to both the valid and good/bad SLI queries. If the `FROM` clause or the `SELECT` function is not the same for both queries, common attributes won't be available and this step is automatically disabled.

   <Callout variant="tip">
     Only attributes that exist in both your valid events and good/bad events queries appear in the **FACET** dropdown. Start with the most high-impact dimensions to keep your views actionable. Avoid selecting attributes with high cardinality, such as `userId` or `traceId`, as they may make the visualization difficult to interpret.
   </Callout>

   <img
     title="Group by attributes configuration"
     alt="Screenshot showing the Group by attributes step in service level configuration"
     src="/images/slm_screenshot-crop_group-by-attributes.webp"
   />

5. If you're creating a new Service Level:

   1. [Set the SLO time window and target](/docs/service-level-management/create-slm/#set-slo).
   2. [Name and tag your SLI](/docs/service-level-management/create-slm/#define-sli).

6. Save the service level.

<Callout variant="important">
  Compliance and error budgets for facets are calculated from the moment of configuration forward. Historical data is not backfilled or recalculated for facets added to existing service levels.
</Callout>
