## Mobile runtimes [#mobile-runtimes]

For support of Android runtimes, New Relic follows [Google-Android EOL policies](https://endoflife.date/android).

For support of iOS, iPadOS, watchOS and tvOS runtimes, New Relic follows [Apple EOL policies](https://support.apple.com/).
