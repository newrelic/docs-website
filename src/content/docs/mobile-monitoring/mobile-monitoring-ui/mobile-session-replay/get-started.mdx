Session replay eliminates the guesswork in troubleshooting by providing a video replay of what end users saw on their phone or tablet, synchronized with all the telemetry data from that session. This means you can quickly assess the real impact of errors on your users, identify performance bottlenecks and UX friction points, and resolve issues faster without relying on bug reports or local reproduction.

* [Configure privacy settings](/docs/mobile-monitoring/mobile-monitoring-ui/mobile-session-replay/configure-privacy-settings): Customize masking and data protection options.

* [Manage session replay & modify capabilities](/docs/mobile-monitoring/mobile-monitoring-ui/mobile-session-replay/manage-session-replay): Control who can view replays and modify settings.
* [Advanced session replay](/docs/mobile-monitoring/mobile-monitoring-ui/mobile-session-replay/advanced-session-replay): Use AI summaries, sequence filters, manual recording APIs, and NerdGraph configuration.
