Before you [install and configure](/docs/mobile-monitoring/new-relic-mobile-ios/tvos/tvos-installation-configuration) the tvOS agent, make sure your tvOS app meets these requirements.

## tvOS requirements [#requirements]

Make sure your tvOS app meets these requirements:
