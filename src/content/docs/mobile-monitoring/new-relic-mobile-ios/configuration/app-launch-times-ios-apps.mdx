With the iOS agent, you can report app launch time, also known as "cold time" and "hot time."  Note:  Warm and prewarmed times are not reported.

## View app launch time [#app-launch-time]

<Callout variant="important">
This feature is available for agent versions 7.4.0 and higher.
</Callout>

Your app launch time is reported with the `AppLaunch/Cold` and `AppLaunch/Hot` metrics, which can be viewed in the Metric Explorer in NR1.
Note: App Launch times are not reported when using Simulators or during debugging.
