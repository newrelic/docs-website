New Relic  Queues and Streams offers a comprehensive solution for monitoring Kafka clusters, providing detailed insights into potential issues such as failures, bottlenecks, and throttling.
This solution delivers comprehensive metrics for clusters, partitions, brokers, and topics, enabling proactive management of your message queues and streams ecosystem.

## Getting started with Kafka monitoring

To begin using New Relic's Queues and Streams, you must first integrate New Relic with your message and queues ecosystem. 

Currently, the follwing integrations are available:

* [Amazon Managed Streaming for Apache Kafka(MSK)](/docs/message-queues-streaming/amazon-msk-integration/introduction)
