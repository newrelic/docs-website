The HIPAA Privacy Rule provides federal protections for personal health information held by covered entities, and gives patients an array of rights with respect to that information. Find HIPAA account enablement information at the [HIPAA readiness page](/docs/security/security-privacy/compliance/hipaa-readiness-new-relic/).

<Callout variant="important">
  To use New Relic's offering in compliance with US HIPAA regulations, contact your account representative regarding a Business Associate Addendum (BAA).
</Callout>

## Applicable document by service [#applicable-services]

<Callout variant="caution">
  Not all [New Relic Observability Platform](/docs/new-relic-one/use-new-relic-one/get-started/introduction-new-relic-one/) services are in compliance with the HIPAA-compliant program. For more information, please see [services included](/docs/security/security-privacy/compliance/hitrust/).
</Callout>
