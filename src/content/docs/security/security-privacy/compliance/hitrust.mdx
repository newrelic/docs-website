HITRUST Alliance is a not-for-profit organization whose mission is to champion programs that safeguard sensitive information and manage information risk for organizations across all industries and throughout the third-party supply chain. Additional information can be found at [https://hitrustalliance.net](https://hitrustalliance.net).
