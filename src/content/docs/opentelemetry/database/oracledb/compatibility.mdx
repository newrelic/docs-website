Before you begin monitoring your Oracle Database with NRDOT, ensure your environment meets these requirements.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following:

* Valid New Relic [license key](/docs/apis/intro-apis/new-relic-api-keys/#ingest-license-key)
* Supported architecture: Linux with AMD64 and ARM64
* Network connectivity to New Relic OTLP endpoint
* Oracle Database 19c or later
* This integration is available as a part of New Relic public preview program. Check with your Organization Manager to opt in from the [Previews & Trials](https://one.newrelic.com/admin-portal/promotion-management/home) page.

## Next steps

Once you've verified your environment meets these prerequisites:

1. Choose your installation method:
  - [On-host Container Database (CDB) setup](/docs/opentelemetry/database/oracledb/on-host-cdb)
  - [On-host Pluggable Database (PDB) setup](/docs/opentelemetry/database/oracledb/on-host-pdb)
  - [RDS setup](/docs/opentelemetry/database/oracledb/rds)

2. Review available metrics: [Understand what data will be collected](/docs/opentelemetry/database/oracledb/metrics-reference)
