## Explore the internal telemetry in the APM UI [#explore-ui]

To view your collector's internal telemetry, first [configure your collector](/docs/opentelemetry/collector-observability/collector-observability-setup) to emit this data. Then, navigate to **APM & Services &gt; Services - OpenTelemetry &gt; your_collector_name** to explore the collector's entity.

Depending on the components your collector leverages, some of the charts might not be populated, e.g. if you do not receive or export OTLP data via gRPC, RPC charts will be empty.

## New Summary Page [#summary-page]

- Replaces the old APM UI
- Contains charts for overall collector health, receivers, processors, exporters and batching (requires [batchprocessor](https://github.com/open-telemetry/opentelemetry-collector/tree/v0.128.0/processor/batchprocessor)) behavior
- Dedicated chart for [memorylimiter](https://github.com/open-telemetry/opentelemetry-collector/tree/v0.128.0/processor/memorylimiterprocessor) due to its unique failure mode
- Infrastructure relationships and metrics (if configured, see [examples](/docs/opentelemetry/collector-observability/collector-observability-examples))
