To view policy and condition information for a specific [entity (alert target)](/docs/using-new-relic/welcome-new-relic/get-started/glossary#entity), select the **Alert conditions** page from its product page. Then, from the product's **Alert conditions** page, use the available tools to search, sort, view, or update the alert conditions and their policies.

<CollapserGroup>
  <Collapser
    id="apm"
    title="APM &#38; services"
  >
    To view policy and condition information:

    * [App](/docs/apm/new-relic-apm/installation-configuration/view-apps-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > APM & services > (select an app) > Settings > Alert conditions**.
    * [Key transaction](/docs/apm/transactions/key-transactions/view-key-transactions-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Key transactions > (select a key transaction) > Settings > Alert conditions**.
    * OpenTelemetry: For services instrumented with OpenTelemetry, see our [best practices](/docs/more-integrations/open-source-telemetry-integrations/opentelemetry/best-practices/opentelemetry-best-practices-alerts/#see-alerts-list) guide for help viewing your existing alert conditions.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="browser"
    title="Browser"
  >
    To view policy and condition information for a [browser app](/docs/browser/new-relic-browser/installation-configuration/view-browser-apps-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Browser > (select a browser app) > Settings > Alert conditions**.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="infrastructure"
    title="Infrastructure"
  >
    To view policy and condition information (up to 50) for [infrastructure metrics and hosts:](/docs/infrastructure/new-relic-infrastructure/infrastructure-alert-conditions/infrastructure-alerts-add-edit-or-view-host-alert-information) Go to **[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Infrastructure > Settings**.

    You can also search for specific conditions from the infrastructure UI, or select the **alerts** link on that page to go directly to those policies.
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="mobile"
    title="Mobile"
  >
    To view policy and condition information for a [specific mobile app](/docs/mobile-monitoring/mobile-monitoring-installation/getting-started/view-mobile-apps-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com > All capabilities](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Mobile > (select a mobile app) > Settings > Alert conditions**.
  </Collapser>
</CollapserGroup>
