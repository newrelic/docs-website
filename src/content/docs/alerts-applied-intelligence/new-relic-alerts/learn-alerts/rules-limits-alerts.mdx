This document contains some technical rules and limits for alerts.

## Permission levels [#permissions-rights]

Permissions differ depending on whether you're on our original [product-based pricing model](/docs/accounts/original-accounts-billing/product-pricing/product-based-pricing) or our [New Relic One pricing model](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-users/pricing-billing):

<CollapserGroup>
  <Collapser
    id="new-pricing"
    title="New Relic One pricing model"
  >
    See [Users and roles](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-users/users-roles).
  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="original-pricing"
    title="Original pricing model"
  >
    For accounts on our original [product-based pricing model](/docs/accounts/original-accounts-billing/product-pricing/product-based-pricing), the [user role](/docs/accounts-partnerships/accounts/account-setup/users-roles#alert-rights) determines the Alerts features available:

    * Owner and Admins can add, change, disable, and delete alert policies, conditions, and channels.
    * Users and Restricted Users can only view alert policies and conditions and their settings.
    * Any user role can acknowledge an incident or close a violation.
  </Collapser>
</CollapserGroup>

## Limits

If your organization has a [parent/child account structure](/docs/accounts/original-accounts-billing/original-users-roles/mastersub-account-structure), **child accounts do not inherit a parent account's alert policies**: You must create policies separately for all child accounts.

The following rules apply both to the New Relic One user interface and to the REST API (v2).
