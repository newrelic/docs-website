To view [incidents](/docs/new-relic-solutions/get-started/glossary/#alert-incident) for a specific [entity (alert target)](/docs/using-new-relic/welcome-new-relic/get-started/glossary#alert-entity), select the **Incidents** index from the product page. Then, from the product's **Incidents** index, use the available tools to search, sort, or view incidents for the selected entity. From here you can also view or update the policies and conditions that triggered the incidents.

<img
  title="Screen APM incidents"
  alt="Screen APM incidents"
  src={accountsApmViolationsScreen}
/>

<figcaption>
  Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > All entities** and select an app. Under the **Events** section, click **Issues & activity**, and then select the **Incidents** tab to open the incidents index. Use the available tools to search, sort, or view incidents for the selected entity, or to view or update the associated policies and conditions.
</figcaption>

<CollapserGroup>
  <Collapser
    id="apm"
    title="APM"
  >
    To view incidents for a specific app or key transaction:

    * [App](/docs/apm/new-relic-apm/installation-configuration/view-apps-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > APM & services** and select an app. Under the **Events** section, click **Issues & activity**, and then select the **Incidents** tab.
    * [Key transaction:](/docs/apm/transactions/key-transactions/view-key-transactions-alert-information) Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Key transactions** and select a transaction. Under the **Events** section, click **Issues & activity**, and then select the **Incidents** tab.

  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="browser"
    title="Browser"
  >

    To view incidents for a specific [browser app](/docs/browser/new-relic-browser/installation-configuration/view-browser-apps-alert-information): Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Browser > (select a browser app) > Move views > Event explorer**.

  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="infrastructure"
    title="Infrastructure"
  >

    To view incidents for a [specific host monitored by infrastructure monitoring:](/docs/infrastructure/new-relic-infrastructure/infrastructure-alert-conditions/infrastructure-alerts-add-edit-or-view-host-alert-information) Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Infrastructure > Settings > Alerts**.

  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="mobile"
    title="Mobile"
  >

    To view incidents for a [specific mobile app:](/docs/mobile-monitoring/mobile-monitoring-installation/getting-started/view-mobile-apps-alert-information) Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Mobile > (select a mobile app) > More views > Event explorer**.

  </Collapser>

  <Collapser
    id="synthetics"
    title="Synthetic monitoring"
  >

    To view incidents for a [specific monitor:](/docs/synthetics/new-relic-synthetics/dashboards/view-monitors-alert-information) Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/all-capabilities) > Synthetic monitoring > (select a monitor) > More views > Event explorer**.

  </Collapser>
</CollapserGroup>
