### Notes

* Support for [synthetic monitors](/docs/synthetics/new-relic-synthetics/getting-started/new-relic-synthetics)! You can now use your iOS device to view the health and status of all your current monitors, and view detailed information about specific failures. For more information, see [Synthetics in the iOS app](/docs/mobile-apps/new-relic-ios-app/features/ios-app-features#synthetics).

### New features

* View individual synthetic monitors.
* View failure details for your synthetic monitors.

### Improvements

* Improvements to restoring the view of your app or plugin view after app exit.
