---
title: Plurals
---

Pluralization of nouns results in subtle changes for punctuation and spelling that can impact the way people interpret written words.

## Basics

* Use apostrophes to make single letters plural.
* Don't use apostrophes to make acronyms plural.

| Do                                               | Don't                                          |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------- |
| There are 2 u's in the word vacuum.              | There are 2 us in the word vacuum.             |
| Don't forget to dot your i's and cross your t's. | Don't forget to dot your Is and cross your Ts. |
| Let's talk about the ABCs of IPs.                | Let's talk about the ABC's of IP's.            |
