    * Improve your customers' experience and make data-driven business decisions.
    * Monitor data from detailed dashboards in [APM](/docs/apm/new-relic-apm/getting-started/introduction-new-relic-apm), [infrastructure](/docs/infrastructure/new-relic-infrastructure/getting-started/introduction-new-relic-infrastructure), and other New Relic products.
    * Query, visualize, and share your data via [dashboards](/docs/query-your-data/explore-query-data/dashboards/introduction-dashboards/).

    To keep your agent up to date and ensure you have access to the latest features, see the [Go agent release notes](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/go-release-notes).
