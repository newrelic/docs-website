---
title: "From CloudALM API Instance"
metaDescription: "Obtain service key from CloudALM API Instance"
freshnessValidatedDate: never
---

<Steps>
   <Step>
      Log in to the **CALM subaccount**, then click **Instances and Subscriptions**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create** at the top right.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      In the pop-up screen, select:
         - **Service**: SAP Cloud ALM API
         - **Plan**: Standard
         - **Runtime Environment**: Cloud Foundry
         - **Space**: Check with your BTP administrator
         - **Instance Name**: Follow your organization naming convention

      <img
         title="SAP Cloud ALM API"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the SAP Cloud ALM API"
         src="/images/sap-cloud-alm-api.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Next**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Review and insert required scopes from [Managing Scopes](https://help.sap.com/docs/cloud-alm/apis/managing-scopes) along with the instance name under the parameters tab.

      <img
         title="Manage Scopes"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the Manage Scopes"
         src="/images/manage-scope.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Next** to review the details.

      <img
         title="Review Details"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the Review Details"
         src="/images/cloud-alm-new-instance.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Once created, locate the service under the **Instances** tab.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click the ellipsis (`...`) next to your instance, then click **Create Service Key**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Enter a Service Key Name and **Key Type**: `ClientId/Secret`.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click the ellipsis (`...`) next to your service key and click **Download**.
   </Step>
</Steps>
