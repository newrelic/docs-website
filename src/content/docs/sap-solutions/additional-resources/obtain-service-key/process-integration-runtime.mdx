---
title: "From BTP - process integration runtime instance"
metaDescription: "Obtain service key from BTP - process integration runtime instance"
freshnessValidatedDate: never
---

<Steps>
   <Step>
      Log in to the **BTP subaccount**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      In the **BTP cockpit**, click **Instances and Subscriptions**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create** at the top right.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      In the pop-up screen, select:
         - **Service**: SAP Process Integration Runtime.
         - **Plan**: API.
         - **Runtime Environment**: Cloud Foundry.
         - **Space**: Check with your BTP administrator.
         - **Instance Name**: Follow your organization naming convention.

      <img
         title="Process Integration Instance"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the Process Integration Instance"
         src="/images/process-integration-instance.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Next**, then select:
         - **Roles**: `MonitoringDataRead`
         - **Grant-types**: Default Client Credentials (confirm with your BTP administrator and security team if other types are used).

      <img
         title="BTP Roles and Grant Types"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the BTP Roles and Grant Types"
         src="/images/btp-roles-grant-types.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Next** to review the details.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Once created, locate the service under the **Instances** tab.

      <img
         title="BTP Instance"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the BTP Instance"
         src="/images/btp-instance.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click the ellipsis (`...`) next to your instance, then click **Create Service Key**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Enter a **Service Key Name** and **Key Type**: `ClientId/Secret`.

      <img
         title="BTP New Service Key"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the BTP New Service Key"
         src="/images/btp-new-service-key.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click **Create**.
   </Step>
   <Step>
      Click the ellipsis (`...`) next to your service key and click **Download**.

      <img
         title="BTP Download Service Key"
         alt="A screenshot displaying the BTP Download Service Key"
         src="/images/btp-download.webp"
         width="600"
      />
   </Step>
</Steps>
