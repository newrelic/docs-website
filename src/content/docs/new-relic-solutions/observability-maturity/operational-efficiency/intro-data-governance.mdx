In the context of [our observability maturity series](/docs/new-relic-solutions/observability-maturity/introduction), **Data ingest governance** refers to the practice of ensuring optimal value for the telemetry data your organization has collected. This practice is especially important for complex organizations that have numerous business units and working groups. 

## Before you start [#before-start]

This guide contains detailed recommendations for optimizing your data ingest. Before using this guide, we recommend you review [our general data management docs](/docs/data-apis/manage-data/manage-your-data).

## Desired outcome [#desired-outcome]

Ensure a high return on investment in telemetry data, via these benefits: 

* Noise reduction: Reduce redundant or unnecessary data with confidence.
* Increased transparency: Understand exactly which groups within the organization are contributing which data. Expose where your telemetry data is coming from and reduce data management silos.
* Cost optimization and predictability: Account for ingest costs in a clear and concise way. Adjust your ingest volume with full context of the observability value of the data.
