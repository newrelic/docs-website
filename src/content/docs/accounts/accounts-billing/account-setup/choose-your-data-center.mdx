New Relic's global data hosting structure consists of multiple regions to serve customers worldwide: the United States (US) region, the European Union (EU) region, and the Japan (JP) region.

## Requirements

Access to New Relic's regional data centers requires the latest versions of our agents.

* For new customers: [Install the most recent agent version](/docs/apm/agents/manage-apm-agents/installation/install-agent).
* For existing customers: [Update to the most recent agent version](/docs/apm/agents/manage-apm-agents/installation/update-new-relic-agent).

Minimum <InlinePopover type="apm"/> agent version required:

* [Go 2.0.0](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/go-release-notes) or higher
* [Java 4.0.0](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/java-release-notes) or higher
* [.NET 8.0.0](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/net-release-notes) or higher
* [Node.js 3.0.0](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/nodejs-release-notes) or higher
* [PHP 8.0.0.204](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/php-release-notes) or higher
* [Python 3.0.0.89](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/python-release-notes) or higher
* [Ruby 5.0.0.342](/docs/release-notes/agent-release-notes/ruby-release-notes) or higher

## Regions and availability [#regions-availability]

Your choice of data center is not limited by your geographic location. You can choose to create an organization with data hosted in the US, EU, or JP region, regardless of where you or your systems reside. You are solely responsible for the selection of the appropriate processing and storage region based on any data localization or data sovereignty laws, regulations, and any other third-party legal requirements applicable to you.

Costs for data ingest are dependent on your data center region. For details, see [our pricing page](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-billing).

## Cross-region accounts [#account-structure]

When you sign up for New Relic, a [New Relic organization](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/account-structure/new-relic-account-structure) is created. This is what contains all your accounts and data. When creating an organization, you must specify the data center region.
By default, a New Relic organization and its accounts can only be in a single data center region. 

## Create a regional organization [#create-regional-account]

To create a New Relic organization in a specific region:

1. Go to the New Relic [signup page](https://newrelic.com/signup/).

   OR

   If you have a specific offer from a New Relic partner, follow that link directly.
2. Follow the online steps to create your account.
3. From the <DNT>**Select your region**</DNT> dropdown, select your preferred region (e.g., <DNT>**United States**</DNT>, <DNT>**European Union**</DNT>, or <DNT>**Japan**</DNT>).
4. Agree to the [Terms of Service](https://newrelic.com/terms "Link opens in a new window").
5. When you receive an email confirmation message, select the link to confirm your account and sign in to New Relic. Then [install](/docs/apm/agents/manage-apm-agents/installation/install-agent) or [update to](/docs/apm/agents/manage-apm-agents/installation/update-new-relic-agent) the most recent agent version.

## Billing and pricing [#billing-pricing]

New Relic's [account billing process and pricing options](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-billing) are the same for all regions.

## Operational access and processing [#data-retention]

[Customer Data](/docs/licenses/license-information/product-definitions/new-relic-one-pricing-definitions#customer-data) is hosted in the region selected during account creation. [Systems Operations Data](https://newrelic.com/termsandconditions/services-notices) is stored in the US region. All other information, including account information (such as license subscription information, billing, and internal monitoring) is hosted in the US region and replicated in the EU region.

New Relic may access and process Customer Data in the United States and such other jurisdictions where New Relic has affiliates and subsidiaries, including as may be necessary to maintain, secure, or perform the services, to provide technical support, or as necessary to comply with law or a binding order of a government body.

Customer Data from existing New Relic accounts cannot be transferred or shared across regions, and new data generated cannot be shared with existing accounts.

## Verify your account region [#verifying-account]

Use these options to verify which region your account data is hosted in:

* **Check your URL:** In APM, mouse over the application name to view the URL:
  * If it begins with `rpm.eu.newrelic.com/`, it's an EU-based account
  * If it begins with `rpm.jp.newrelic.com/`, it's a JP-based account
  * If it begins with `rpm.newrelic.com/`, it's a US-based account
* **Check your license key:** Your <InlinePopover type="licenseKey"/> indicates your region:
  * If it begins with `EU`, it's an EU-based account
  * If it begins with `JP`, it's a JP-based account
  * If it has no regional prefix, it's a US-based account
