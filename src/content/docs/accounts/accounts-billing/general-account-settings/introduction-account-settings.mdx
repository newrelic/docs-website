To view and edit your account settings, go to [one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com), select the [account dropdown](/docs/using-new-relic/welcome-new-relic/get-started/glossary#account-dropdown) and select one of the options.

For docs on specific account settings, see the [Accounts and billing](/docs/accounts) category.
