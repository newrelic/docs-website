Effective cloud cost management is crucial for organizations. New Relic offers deep visibility into your CCU usage and alerts. However, implementing proactive mechanisms to control and stay within budget is essential for managing costs effectively across the organization.

## Manage limits

<Steps>

  <Step>
    ## Access compute budgets
    To access compute budgets, go to New Relic. In the [user menu](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/general-account-settings/intro-account-settings), click <DNT>**Administration**</DNT> > <DNT>**Compute Budgets**</DNT>.

    <Callout title="important">
      By default, the **Compute Budgets** landing page displays the default **Organization budget**, which includes the **Intelligent Observability (advanced CCU)** and **Full Stack Observability (core CCU)** budgets, based on your CCU commitments.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Manage budgets
    On the **Compute Budgets** page, click **Manage limits**. The **Manage Organization Budget** page appears.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Specify CCU commitments
    1. In the **Set your organization budget** section, specify the amount of advanced CCU and core CCU commitments you want to track in your budget.
    2. Click **Next** to set up notifications.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Set up notifications
    1. On the **Set up notifications** page, edit the default notification settings to prevent overages and manage costs effectively by configuring the budget threshold percentage and notification type.
    2. Click **Add notification** to add an additional notification for the budget. Configure the following options:
       - **Budget threshold**: Specify the percentage at which you want to be notified.
       - **Notification type**: Choose from the dropdown list.
       - **Notify people who aren't on this budget**: Toggle this option on to notify individuals who are not included in the budget.
       - **Email addresses**: Optionally, add email addresses of New Relic users to notify them about the budget.
    3. Click **Add notification** to save the notification settings. Once saved, the custom notification appears on the **Set up notifications** page.
    4. Click **Next** to save the budget settings.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ## Save the budget settings
    Click **Next** to save the budget settings.
  </Step>
</Steps>
