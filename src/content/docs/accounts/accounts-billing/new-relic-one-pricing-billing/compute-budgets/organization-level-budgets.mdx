New Relic offers deep visibility into your CCU usage and alerts. However, implementing proactive mechanisms to control and stay within budget is essential for managing costs effectively across the organization.

## Manage organization level budgets limits

<Steps>

  <Step>
    ### Access compute budgets

    Go to **[one.newrelic.com](https://one.newrelic.com/)**. In the [user menu](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/general-account-settings/intro-account-settings), click **Administration** > **Compute Budgets**.

    <Callout title="important">
      When you open the **Compute Budgets** landing page, the **Organization Budget** appears by default. This budget reflects the SKUs you've purchased and updates monthly. It provides a clear overview of your Compute Consumption Unit (CCU) commitments across these SKUs, helping you manage and monitor your compute usage effectively.
    </Callout>
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Manage budget limits

    In the **Organization Budget** section, click **Manage limits**. The **Edit Organization Budget** page appears.
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set montly budget limits

    1. To set spending thresholds for your selected account, go to the **Set monthly budget limits** section and set the limits for the compute SKUs you've purchased to establish your `Org budget`.
  
    <Callout variant="important">
      By default we are setting it up depending on your monthly contract commitments.
    </Callout>
    
    2. Click **Next**.

    <img
      title="Screenshot displaying the Set monthly budget limits page with advanced and core CCU limits highlighted"
      alt="Screenshot displaying the Set monthly budget limits page with advanced and core CCU limits highlighted"
      src="/images/set-monthly-budget-limits.webp"
    />
  </Step>

  <Step>
    ### Set up notifications

    1. To prevent overages and manage costs effectively, go to the **Set up organization budget notifications** page. Set the threshold percentage and choose the destination for notifications.
    2. To receive a warning when you've used a certain percentage of the CCU assigned to a budget for a specific month, go to the **Thresholds** tab. Select **+ Add a threshold** and enter the percentage at which you want to receive notifications.
    3. To ensure notifications are sent when the budget exceeds the threshold, go to the **Destinations** tab. Add at least one person to be notified. You can choose from the following options:
        - **Email**: Enter the email addresses of New Relic users to notify them about the budget.
        - **Webhook**: Enter the webhook URL to send notifications to a specific endpoint.
    4. Click **Finish**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
