The <DNT>**Consumption Management**</DNT> page shows exactly how your organization uses its Compute Consumption Units (CCUs). This guide shows you how to track, analyze, and manage your CCU usage.

<Callout variant="tip">

  <DNT>**Consumption Management**</DNT> is available to all customers on [Advanced or Core Compute plans](/docs/accounts/accounts-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-billing/new-relic-one-pricing-billing) within their New Relic account.

</Callout>

## Access Consumption Management [#access-consumption-management]

To access <DNT>**Consumption Management**</DNT>:
1. Go to **[New Relic Administration](https://one.newrelic.com/admin-portal)**
2. Select <DNT>**Consumption Management**</DNT>

## Break down your usage [#break-down-your-usage]

Use the <DNT>**See usage for**</DNT> dropdown to view different types of compute usage:
  * <DNT>**Core Compute**</DNT>: View your organization's core compute usage.
  * <DNT>**Advanced Compute Bundle**</DNT>: View your organization's advanced compute usage.

By default, the page displays data from the last 30 days. You can change this to 60 or 90 days using the time selector in the top-right corner.

Use the dynamic chart controls to focus on specific entities: select a category (Accounts, Users, or Capabilities) from the dropdown, search for specific entities, or apply filters to narrow your view.

<Callout variant="tip">
While you can group the dynamic chart data by daily, weekly, or monthly intervals, when adding it to a dashboard, select <DNT>**Auto**</DNT> grouping to let the system optimize the display based on the dashboard's time range.
</Callout>

The donut charts show the number and percentage of CCUs contributed by capabilities, users, and accounts for the selected month. Filters do not affect these charts.

## Switch between billable and non-billable CCU views [#switch-between-billable-and-non-billable-ccu-views]

By default, the page displays billable CCU data. To view non-billable CCU data, turn on the <DNT>**Non-billable CCU**</DNT> toggle.

## Manage and share your usage data [#manage-and-share-your-usage-data]

From the <Icon name="fe-more-horizontal"/> menu on any chart, you can:

* Add the chart to your [dashboards](/docs/query-your-data/explore-query-data/dashboards/manage-your-dashboard/) for easy monitoring.
* [Set up an alert](/docs/tutorial-create-alerts/create-an-alert/) to notify you of significant changes in usage.
* [Share a real-time view](/docs/query-your-data/explore-query-data/dashboards/share-charts-dashboards-externally/#chart) of your data with stakeholders.
* Save the chart as an image for presentations or reports.
* View the underlying query that powers the chart, which you can use for your own custom reports or deeper analysis.

## Forecast your CCU usage

The <DNT>**Consumption Management**</DNT> page includes predictive capabilities that help you forecast your organization's future compute usage.

To forecast usage:
1. Navigate to the <DNT>**Daily view**</DNT> of the time series chart at the top of the page
2. From the chart menu, select <DNT>**Predict usage**</DNT>
3. View the CCU usage forecast for 1 week in both the chart and the table

To remove the forecast, select <DNT>**Remove prediction**</DNT> from the chart menu.

<Callout variant="tip">
Forecasting is only available for <DNT>**Advanced Compute**</DNT> customers and only in the <DNT>**Daily view**</DNT> of the <DNT>**Consumption Management time series chart**</DNT>.
</Callout>
