Promotions are special offers to try out New Relic product features. You can browse through our promotions and manage your enrolment on the <DNT>**Previews & trials**</DNT> page. 

The current available promotion is product previews, which lets you try a feature before its official release. From the **<DNT>Previews & trials</DNT>** page you can get:

* **Centralized access**: View all available product previews in one location.
* **Enrollment flexibility**: Join or leave product previews until theyâ€™re generally available (GA).
* **Upgrade options**: Upgrade to access products when they reach GA.

## Eligibility [#eligibility]
To check the available product previews for your organization, navigate to <DNT>**[one.newrelic.com > Administration](https://one.newrelic.com/admin-portal) > Preview & Trials**</DNT>. If the product preview for which you want to enroll is not available, your organization may not be eligible for that. Reach out to your billing admin to enroll your organization in the product preview.

### Eligible users [#users]

Product previews are available to the entire organization, covering all users and accounts within it. The enrollment options may vary depending on the product preview. Some previews allow any user within the organization to enroll, while others limit enrollment, leaving, or re-enrollment to billing users only.

### Eligible organizations [#org-rules]

Organizations can enroll in multiple product previews concurrently. Eligibility for a preview depends on various factors. If you get an error message while trying to opt into a promotion, reach out to your billing admin. You may need to perform additional steps for enrollment, or your organization may not be eligible for the preview.

## Manage your enrollment [#access-ui]

The <DNT>**Previews & trials**</DNT> page lists available product preview in a single, consolidated table view.

<Callout variant="important">
  Some product previews don't have a defined end date, so you may get the end date as <DNT>**Ongoing**</DNT>. We try to provide a best estimate of the length of the preview, but it is provided for informational purposes only. The end date should not be construed as an offer, commitment, promise or obligation on behalf of New Relic to deliver any product, material, code, functionality, or other feature.
</Callout>

To sign up for a promotion or to leave one you're currently enrolled in: 

1. Go to <DNT>**[one.newrelic.com > Administration](https://one.newrelic.com/admin-portal) > Preview & Trials**</DNT>.
2. From the list of available promotions, select the one you want to enroll in or leave.
3. To enroll for the promotion, accept the terms and conditions and click <DNT>**Accept & enroll**</DNT>. To leave the promotion, click **Leave Preview**.

## Upgrade after preview feature is released [#upgrade-at-ga]

After a product preview transitions to GA, you'll receive an in-product notification. To continue accessing the feature, perform the following steps:

1. On the notification, click **Manage preview**.
