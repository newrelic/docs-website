## Change user name [#user-name]

To change your user name:

1. From the [account dropdown](/docs/accounts-partnerships/education/getting-started-new-relic/glossary#account-dropdown), select **User preferences**.
2. On the right side, click **Full name**.

## Change account name [#account-name]

Only users on our [original user model](/docs/accounts/original-accounts-billing/original-product-based-pricing/overview-changes-pricing-user-model#user-models) are able to change their New Relic account name. To do this:

1. From the [account dropdown](/docs/accounts-partnerships/education/getting-started-new-relic/glossary#account-dropdown), select **Account settings**.
2. On the right side, edit the **Name** field.

<Callout variant="important">
  If you're on our [New Relic One user model](/docs/accounts/original-accounts-billing/original-product-based-pricing/overview-changes-pricing-user-model#user-models) and want to change your account name, contact your account representative or [support](/docs/using-new-relic/welcome-new-relic/get-started/find-help-use-support-portal#file-ticket).
</Callout>
